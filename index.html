About Me

Programming is my passion. I've been programming for 7 years in a variety of languages such as Java, C++, and Javascript and HTML. I'm especially interested in back-end programming and games. I'm an innovative, hard-working, and great at solving problems creatively. I love to experiment and try new ways of doing things, and so I'm frequently rewriting past code to try and improve on what I already have, or even just to try something new and see if it works better. I'm also a fast learner, and I'm always looking to learn new things!

I am a nonbinary individual, and my pronouns are they/them. I aim to bring diversity and a unique and different perspective to whatever team I work with in an effort to make the tech field a more welcoming and accommodating place.

I'm always working on something new, so check out my GitHub if you'd like to stay updated on my projects or see my past projects!
